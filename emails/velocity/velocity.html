Velocity Fitness Promotional Email

Fully responsive promotional email created with HTML and CSS for a fictional fitness company. Top section image created in Adobe Photoshop. Purpose is to inform customers of currecnt sale as well as inform them of additional products offered. Email was tested with Putsmail and functioned as intended on majority of email clients. However the sections with dark backgrounds can show white backgrounds in the Gmail app if the user has his system set to Dark Mode.
